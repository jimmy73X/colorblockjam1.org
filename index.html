What is Color Block Jam?

An engaging puzzle game where you slide colorful blocks to match them with same-colored gates

Game Overview

Color Block Jam is an addictive puzzle game that tests your strategic thinking and color matching abilities. The goal is simple: slide colored blocks to their matching gates while navigating around obstacles.

Each level presents new challenges with increasing complexity. Plan your moves carefully, as one wrong slide can block your path to victory!

How to Play

Master the simple yet challenging gameplay of Color Block Jam

1

Slide the Blocks

Click and drag blocks to slide them in any direction. Blocks will move until they hit an obstacle or wall.

2

Match Colors

Guide each colored block to the matching colored gate. All blocks must be matched to complete the level.

3

Avoid Obstacles

Navigate around walls, barriers and special obstacles that block your path.

4

Plan Your Strategy

Think ahead! The order you move blocks affects your ability to complete the puzzle.
